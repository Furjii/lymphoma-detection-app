About Lymphoma Classification

Lymphoma is a type of cancer that affects the lymphatic system, which is part of the body's germ-fighting network. This AI-powered system analyzes microscopic images to classify lymphoma into three major types based on cell morphology and pattern recognition.

Please note: This system is designed as a decision support tool and should be used in conjunction with professional medical evaluation.
